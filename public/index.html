About us

We are on a mission to create a safer everyday ride for millions of two-wheeled riders around the globe.

In a world where millions die in two-wheeler road accidents every year, we are re-inventing motorbike riding awareness by designing Hamlit- World's first machine learning-based blind spot notifier.

Comprising a team of engineers, designers, entrepreneurs but most importantly a few passionate motorbike riders, we will be launching our first product from Singapore in early 2020.
